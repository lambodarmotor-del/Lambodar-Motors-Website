Car Servicing Interval in Ahmedabad's Heat & Dust

Wondering how often to service your car in Gota, Ahmedabad's extreme heat and dust? Get Shailesh Bhai's servicing advice here.

Ahmedabad is known for its heritage, but it's also known for extreme summer heat and dusty Gota roads. These conditions constitute 'severe driving conditions' in your car manual. Let's understand how often your gaadi needs servicing under these local conditions.

1. Why the 10,000 km Rule Doesn't Always Apply

Most manufacturers recommend a service every 10,000 km or 1 year. However, if you drive in Gota, Chandkheda, or SG Highway traffic, your engine runs even when you are standing still. This engine-idling time combined with thick dust degrades engine oil much faster.

If your daily commute involves heavy city traffic, shifting your service interval to 7,500 km or 6 months is much safer for engine health. This prevents carbon buildup and sludge inside the engine block.

2. The Role of Air Filters in Dusty Climates

Gota has high levels of airborne dust due to ongoing constructions. Your car's air filter is its lungs. A choked air filter restricts airflow, forcing the engine to burn more fuel to maintain performance.

At Lambodar Motors, we clean the air filter at every service and recommend replacing it every 10,000 km without fail. Clean filters keep your fuel economy high and your drive smooth.

3. Protecting Your Coolant and Battery

Our summer temperatures easily cross 45°C. This extreme heat degrades your engine coolant and accelerates battery fluid evaporation. Low coolant levels lead to engine overheating, which can wrap your cylinder head.

Regular checks of fluid levels and battery voltage are essential. It takes just 5 minutes but saves you from engine failures in the middle of a hot afternoon.

Pro Tip from Shailesh Bhai: If you drive less than 5,000 km a year, you still need to change your engine oil once a year. Engine oil loses its chemical properties over time even if the car is stationary!

Frequently Asked Questions

Q: What happens if I delay my car service?

Delaying service causes the engine oil to turn into thick sludge, which fails to lubricate parts and can cause permanent engine wear.

Q: Should I choose mineral or synthetic engine oil?

Synthetic oil is highly recommended for Ahmedabad's hot climate because it retains its viscosity at high temperatures, unlike mineral oil.

Q: How often should spark plugs be replaced?

Standard spark plugs last about 20,000 km, while iridium plugs can easily go up to 60,000 km before needing replacement.

Q: Why is my car giving less mileage?

Low mileage is commonly caused by dirty air filters, worn spark plugs, low tire pressure, or misaligned wheels.

Q: What is included in a general car service?

A general service includes oil change, oil filter replacement, air and cabin filter cleaning, coolant check, brake cleaning, and a multi-point inspection.
