Privacy Policy

Last updated: July 2026

At Lambodar Motors, we value your privacy. In compliance with India's Digital Personal Data Protection (DPDP) Act 2023, this policy explains how we collect and process information when you visit our website.

1. Information We Collect

We do not run direct registration profiles or member databases on this website. The only personal data we process is details you choose to share with us via our contact form or when you contact us directly via phone or WhatsApp support channels (e.g. name, phone number, vehicle registration number, and service requirements).

2. Google Maps & Third-Party Cookies

This website embeds Google Maps to help visitors find our physical workshop location in Gota, Ahmedabad. Google Maps may drop tracking cookies or store data in your browser for telemetry and map rendering features. You can configure your browser preferences to reject third-party cookies if you choose.

3. WhatsApp Integration

When you click our WhatsApp support buttons, you are redirected to the official WhatsApp application. Your communication occurs directly with our representatives, and your phone number and conversation details are handled confidentially.

4. No Data Sold

We do not sell, rent, lease, or distribute any user or customer data to third-party advertisers. All collected inquiry information is used exclusively to service your vehicle maintenance requests.
