Festival Car Care: Get Ready for Navratri & Diwali | Gota, Ahmedabad

Get your car ready for Navratri, Diwali, and the wedding season in Gota, Ahmedabad. Read Shailesh Bhai's festival car checklist.

Festivals in Ahmedabad are full of joy, dress-ups, Navratri garba nights, and family visits, bhai! Your gaadi is a key part of these celebrations. The last thing you want is a starter failure or a dusty car when arriving at a garba venue or wedding hall. Here is how to keep your car festive-ready.

1. Paint Polish & Interior Cleaning

You dress up in your best clothes for Navratri and Diwali—your car deserves to look good too! Driving in Gota's dust makes your paint look dull. A professional body wash followed by a wax polish restores that festive shine.

Don't forget the interior. Vacuuming the carpets, dry cleaning the fabric seats, and polishing the dashboard creates a clean, pleasant cabin for your family and guests during late-night festival drives.

2. Headlight & Electrical Readiness

Navratri means late nights. You will be driving back from Garba venues at 2:00 AM on busy highways. Make sure your headlights have excellent throw. If your headlight covers look yellow or cloudy, a quick restoration polish will double your night visibility.

Check all fog lights, tail lights, and cabin lights. Replacing dim halogen bulbs with bright LEDs is a great, affordable festival upgrade.

3. Battery & Starting Checkup

Standing in bumper-to-bumper traffic near wedding halls drains your battery due to heavy AC, music, and headlight load. A weak battery will leave you stranded in traffic. Shailesh Prajapati suggests getting your battery health tested before the festival rush starts.

We perform a quick starting system and alternator health check to ensure your car cranks instantly every single time.

Pro Tip from Shailesh Bhai: Apply a high-quality dashboard polish. It doesn't just make the plastic shine; it forms a protective layer that prevents Gota's fine dust from sticking to the surface!

Frequently Asked Questions

Q: How do you clean yellowed headlight lenses?

We sand the oxidized outer layer with fine sandpaper, compound buff it to restore transparency, and apply a UV sealant to keep them clear.

Q: What is the cost of festival car polish?

A high-quality exterior rubbing compound and wax polish package starts at ₹1,500 for hatchbacks at our Gota workshop.

Q: Why does my car AC smell bad when visitors sit?

A dusty cabin air filter holds smells. Replacing it before the festival season ensures clean, fresh-smelling air for your passengers.

Q: Can I get my car washed on Diwali day?

We recommend booking your wash 2-3 days before Diwali, as festival eve is extremely busy and workshops have long wait times.

Q: What is interior deep cleaning?

Interior deep cleaning includes shampooing the roof lining, seat dry cleaning, door pad scrubbing, vacuuming, and cleaning the AC vents.
