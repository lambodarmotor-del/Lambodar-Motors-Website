Service Frequently Asked Questions

Q: How much does car denting and painting cost in Ahmedabad?

At Lambodar Motors, Gota, minor dent repair starts from ₹500 per panel. Full panel repainting starts from ₹1,500 depending on car model, paint grade, and damage size. Multi-panel jobs are priced after a free physical inspection. No advance payment required before estimate.

Q: Can you fix car scratches without full panel repaint?

Yes. Minor surface scratches at Lambodar Motors are treated using scratch removal and paint touch-up techniques without repainting the full panel. Deep scratches that have reached the metal require panel repainting for a factory finish.

Q: Do you provide any warranty on repairs done at Lambodar Motors?

Yes. Lambodar Motors provides workmanship warranty on painting and denting jobs. If paint peeling or dent recurrence is caused by workshop error, it is rectified at no additional cost.
