Frequently Asked Questions

Q: Which insurance companies support cashless claims?

We support cashless claim processing with most leading insurance companies including ICICI Lombard, HDFC ERGO, Bajaj Allianz, Tata AIG, SBI General, National Insurance, New India Assurance, and others.

Q: What documents are required to file a claim?

You will need the vehicle's Registration Certificate (RC), valid Driving License (DL), Insurance Policy copy, and a signed claim form along with the driver's PAN/Aadhaar.

Q: Do I have to pay anything for accident repairs?

You only pay the compulsory deductible (excess) and any depreciation on parts (like plastic, glass, or rubber) if you do not have a zero-depreciation cover.

Q: How long does the surveyor approval take?

Typically, the surveyor inspects the car and approves the estimate within 24 to 48 hours of vehicle arrival and document submission.
