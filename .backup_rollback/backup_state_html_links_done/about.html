About Lambodar Motors

Providing honest, high-quality, and affordable multi-brand car repair and service in Gota, Ahmedabad for over 10+ years.

Our Background

Founded and personally managed by Shailesh Bhai Prajapati, Lambodar Motors has grown into one of the most trusted independent car workshops in Gota, Ahmedabad. With over a decade of hands-on mechanical and detailing experience, Shailesh Bhai and his team operate with a single core value: complete honesty.

The Lambodar Pledge

Unlike many dealerships or franchise chains, we don't suggest arbitrary part replacements or hide labor margins. When you bring your vehicle to Shop No. 12 at Shree Swam Complex, we perform a thorough diagnosis in front of you. You get a pre-work estimate, and that price is exactly what you pay—no surprise surcharges.

Transparent Pricing & Genuine Spare Parts. Always.

Multi-Brand Service Range

We service both petrol, diesel, and CNG variants of all major brands, including Maruti Suzuki, Hyundai, Honda, Tata, Mahindra, Toyota, Renault, Kia, and others. From bodywork restorations (denting & painting in our dedicated booth) to engine overhauls and AC repairs, we use certified diagnostic equipment to ensure factory-perfect finishes.
