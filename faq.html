❄️ AC SPECIFIC

Q: How do I know if my car AC needs a gas refill?

Signs that your car AC needs gas refill include weak cooling even on maximum setting, AC blowing room temperature air, compressor switching on and off rapidly, and visible ice formation on AC pipes. At Lambodar Motors, AC pressure testing is done free to confirm if a refill is required before charging for the service.

Q: Why does my car AC smell bad when turned on?

A bad smell from car AC vents is caused by mold and bacteria growth inside the evaporator core. This happens due to moisture buildup and infrequent AC use. Lambodar Motors offers AC sanitization service that eliminates the bacterial buildup and restores clean cabin air. This is a same-day service.

Q: How long does car AC repair take at Lambodar Motors?

AC gas refill takes 1–2 hours. AC compressor servicing takes 3–5 hours. Full compressor replacement takes 1 day. All timelines are confirmed before work begins. Same-day completion is available for gas refill and minor AC jobs.

Q: How often should car AC be serviced in Ahmedabad?

In Ahmedabad, car AC should be serviced once a year before summer, ideally in February or March. Cabin air filter replacement is recommended every 15,000 km. Due to Ahmedabad's extreme summer heat, earlier servicing is recommended if cooling performance drops noticeably.
