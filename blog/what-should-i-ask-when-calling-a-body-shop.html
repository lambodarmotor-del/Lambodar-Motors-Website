Crucial Qualities of a Reliable Auto Workshop

Finding answers to 'What Should I Ask When Calling a Body Shop?' starts with looking for key indicators of trust. A reliable workshop is always upfront about its repair procedures, uses clean and specialized tools, and has technicians who take the time to explain the issue.
