My Journey

Founder Biography

25+ Years of Dedicated Automotive Service

With over 25 years of experience in the automotive industry, Shailesh Prajapati has dedicated his career to delivering reliable, honest, and high-quality vehicle care. His professional journey began with Maruti Suzuki, where he built a strong technical foundation and continuously expanded his expertise through years of hands-on experience.

During his career, he progressed from a skilled technician to taking on leadership responsibilities as a Workshop Manager, overseeing workshop operations, managing technical teams, maintaining service quality, and ensuring complete customer satisfaction. Over nearly 17 years with Maruti Suzuki, he gained extensive experience in vehicle diagnostics, engine and transmission repairs, suspension systems, electrical troubleshooting, preventive maintenance, and modern automotive technologies.

Throughout these years, he developed strong relationships across the automotive industry, including trusted suppliers and genuine spare parts distributors. This professional network enables Lambodar Motors to source high-quality genuine and OEM parts whenever possible, ensuring customers receive reliable repairs without compromising on quality.

Driven by a passion for automobiles and a desire to offer customers transparent, dependable service, Shailesh Prajapati founded Lambodar Motors. His vision was to create a workshop where every customer receives honest advice, skilled workmanship, fair pricing, and personalized attention rather than unnecessary repairs or hidden costs.

Today, Lambodar Motors continues to operate on the same values that have guided him throughout his career - integrity, technical excellence, transparency, and customer trust. Every vehicle is treated with the same level of care and precision as if it were his own, earning the confidence of customers throughout Ahmedabad.
