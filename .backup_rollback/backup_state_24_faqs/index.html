Frequently Asked Questions

Q: How much does car denting and painting cost in Ahmedabad?

At Lambodar Motors, Gota, minor dent repair starts from ₹500 per panel. Full panel repainting starts from ₹1,500 depending on car model, paint grade, and damage size. Multi-panel jobs are priced after a free physical inspection. No advance payment required before estimate.

Q: Do you give free estimates before starting work?

Yes. Lambodar Motors provides free inspection and cost estimates for all jobs. No charges apply for diagnosis or assessment. Bring your vehicle to Shop No. 12, Shree Swam Complex, Gota or WhatsApp a photo of the damage for a preliminary quote.

Q: How long does car repair take at Lambodar Motors?

Repair time depends on the job. AC gas refill and minor electrical fixes are completed same day. Denting and single panel painting takes 1–2 days. Full car repainting takes 3–5 days. Engine repair timelines are confirmed after diagnosis. All estimated timelines are communicated before work begins.

Q: Which car brands do you service?

Lambodar Motors services all major car brands including Maruti Suzuki, Hyundai, Honda, Tata, Mahindra, Toyota, Kia, Renault, Volkswagen, Skoda, and MG. Both petrol and diesel variants are accepted. No brand restriction applies.

Q: Where exactly is Lambodar Motors located in Gota, Ahmedabad?

Lambodar Motors is located at Shop No. 12, Shree Swam Complex, near Gokul Hotel, beside Gota Cross Road, Vasant Nagar, Gota, Ahmedabad, Gujarat 382481. The easiest landmark is Gokul Hotel — the workshop is directly beside it.
